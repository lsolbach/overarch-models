
# GDPR (Regulation)
## Description
The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) aims to protect the privacy and personal data of individuals.


## Documentation
The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a regulation that
         aims to protect the privacy and personal data of individuals in the
         European Union. It establishes rules for the collection, processing,
         and storage of personal data, and grants individuals certain rights
         regarding their personal data. The GDPR applies to organizations that
         process personal data of individuals in the EU, regardless of where
         the organization is located.
