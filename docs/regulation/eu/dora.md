
# DORA (Regulation)
## Description
The Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) aims to strengthen the digital operational resilience of financial entities.


## Documentation
The Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) is a regulation that
         aims to strengthen the digital operational resilience of financial
         entities. It establishes a comprehensive framework for the management
         of ICT risks, including requirements for ICT risk management, incident
         reporting, digital operational resilience testing, and information
         sharing. DORA applies to a wide range of financial entities, including
         banks, insurance companies, investment firms, and payment service
         providers.
